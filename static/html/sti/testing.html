STD/STI Testing

Sexually Transmitted Disease (STD) and/or Sexually Transmitted Infection (STI)

All sexually active individuals that are not in a monogamous relationship should be tested for a sexually transmitted infection (STI) and/or a sexually transmitted disease (STD). If you are sexually active and have more than one partner you are at risk for STIs. STIs are very common.

We are currently testing for both gonorrhea and chlamydia at no cost to the patient. There are many different types of STIs. Call and talk to our nurse to determine the type of test you need.
