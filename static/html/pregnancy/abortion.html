Verify Your Pregnancy

Before making an appointment for any type of abortion, you will need to verify you are actually pregnant. You will also need to know how far along you are in the pregnancy. Even if you are experiencing pregnancy symptoms or have taken a home pregnancy test that was positive, you should get a medically accurate, lab-quality pregnancy test. You can get a FREE lab-quality pregnancy test at ZoeCare. Our tests are 99% accurate.

Previous Abortion?

It’s important to understand that having more than one abortion brings increases health risks and possible fertility implications.
