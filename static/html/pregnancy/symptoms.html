Pregnancy Signs and Symptoms

Common symptoms of pregnancy include the following:

  • Missed period
  • Spotting
  • Nausea/Morning Sickness
    Nausea commonly occurs in the morning. It can occur if you have not eaten for three or more hours and occasionally some women experience some nausea throughout the day.
  • Breast tenderness and/or enlargement
  • Increased appetite
  • Decreased appetite
  • Fatigue/Tiredness
  • Bloating
  • Dizziness
  • Backache
  • Headache
  • Frequent urination
  • Food cravings or Food aversions
  • Increased sense of smell
  • Moodiness
  • Constipation

Sometimes there are NO noticeable signs or symptoms.

Some of these symptoms like sore breasts, bloating, and moodiness often occur right before your menstrual period.
