Jeanne joined the ZoeCare team in April 2013 as Executive Director. As E.D., Jeanne oversees all operations of ZoeCare. She is focused primarily on providing confidential, compassionate quality care and education to ZoeCare clients. It is Jeanne’s desire that all ZoeCare clients will see and know their intrinsic value as individuals, their potential as person’s made in the image of God, and experience great joy in their individual life journeys. Similarly ZoeCare has an extraordinary staff dedicated to providing unparalleled service. These women are a great joy and make Jeanne’s job the “best job ever!”

Jeanne has a veritable smorgasbord of experiences in a number of industries . . . all of which have helped her learn, grow and develop as a person and leader. Jeanne has a BS in General Studies, from Eastern Oregon University as well as a JD from Gonzaga University School of Law. A voracious reader Jeanne loves to take classes and has also studied at Idaho State University, Montana State University, various community colleges in southern California and SUNY (State University of New York) in Purchase, NY.

Jeanne has taught in both public and private schools, worked in property management, construction, retail, for Washington Department of Transportation, practiced law in Washington state for nearly ten years, including General Counsel of an Olympia based think-tank, and in the insurance industry here in Montana.

Jeanne has five siblings, eighteen nieces and nephews and travels as often as possible to spend time with family and friends. She loves to travel, meet new people, try new foods and see new places from Bozeman to Belgravia, Twin Cities to Timbuktu, Jordan to Jerusalem, Logan to London and all points in between. She enjoys crosswords, cozy mysteries, rambling about in the great outdoors and is on a grand quest for the world’s best dark chocolate!
