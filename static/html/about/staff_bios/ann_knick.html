Ann graduated from MSU in elementary education and enjoyed several years teaching before having three children with her husband, Tony. As their children grew, Ann started spending time in the classroom as a substitute teacher.

As time passed, someone invited her to check out Zoe for an opportunity to volunteer. In the fall of 2007, Ann began to volunteer one morning per week. In 2009 Ann was asked to join the staff training new volunteers.

Currently, Ann works as one of the receptionists, as well as doing data entry and serving patients when needed. In her spare time, she may can be found spending time with grandchildren, working out, or volunteering as a CAP mentor.
