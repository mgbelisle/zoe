Deanne joined the ZoeCare staff in summer 2013 as a volunteer receptionist, working Monday and Wednesday. Deanne brings extraordinary life experiences to ZoeCare including overseas mission ministry work with her husband Keith, and parenting their two daughters, who are both now grown, she also worked previously at the Adult Learning Center. She earned a Bachelor’s Degree in Nursing from Idaho State University and worked as a public health nurse before becoming a mother to her two children.

Everyone will tell you that Deanne is one of the wisest, humble, most compassionate women they have ever known. We at ZoeCare are thrilled she has brought her wisdom and servant’s heart to Zoe.
