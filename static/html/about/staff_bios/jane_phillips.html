Jane received her training as an RN in Iowa. She moved to Montana the following year where she met and later married, her husband John Phillips. She has worked as an RN in acute care settings for the past 39 years. Her nursing experience includes ICU, ER, Day Surgery, and most recently ZoeCare. Jane joined the ZoeCare team in 2003. She became a certified nurse sonographer in 2007 when ZoeCare became a medical facility.

Jane feels so blessed to be given the opportunity to help women in crisis pregnancies as well as teaching young women about the beauty of the gift of their fertility and the importance of sexual integrity. Jane enjoys traveling with John, spending time with their daughters and their families. She loves gardening, hiking and enjoying the beautiful state of Montana.
