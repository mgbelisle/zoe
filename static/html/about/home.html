About ZoeCare

ZoeCare is a faith-based, Christian, nonprofit, 501(c)3 charitable organization established to provide accurate, up to date information to our clients regarding their sexual health. We offer confidential, compassionate, education, options counseling and treatment. All ZoeCare client services are offered FREE of charge.

We provide FREE pregnancy testing, limited ultrasound, STD/STI testing and treatment, contraception information, options counseling including adoption, parenting, and abortion facts and information. We do not provide contraceptives nor do we refer for abortions.

ZoeCare, located in beautiful Bozeman, Montana, was first established in 1978 as the Pregnancy Caring Center of Gallatin County. The center was established as an abortion alternative center to affirm women in their right to know all the facts and options available when they were facing an unintended pregnancy.

Our primary mission is to serve the mother and save the child with medical excellence in the love of CHRIST. It is our desire that no woman or man facing an unplanned pregnancy will have to know or experience the anguish and trauma born of the decision to abort their child.
